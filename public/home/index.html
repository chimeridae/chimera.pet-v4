Welcome to the Animals' Den!

All of a sudden, you feel a looming presence... oh, it's just C H I M E R A ! You've wandered into my den!

I'm a collection of animals who've made their home on the web. Feel free to take a break here and rest your paws. This site serves as my respite and proof of life. It's a playplace for all of the fluffy fuzzy parts of me, both big and small! Don't worry too much if things seem inconsistent... That's part of the fun! Just keep in mind that this site is not intended for those under the age of 18 !

A couple of things you'll see as you wander through here may include:

  • My personal artworks, created for fun outside of business (hey - this site's a form of art, too!)
  • Journals, articles, and other personal writeups and hideouts
  • Fanpages and various resources dedicated to my favorite things!

All of this, and even more is in store... deeper in the depths of Chimera.pet! Take a look around!
