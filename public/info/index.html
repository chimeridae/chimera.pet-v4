Pixel art

Black & White animated sprite

$60* - $80*

A fullbody sprite animated in the puppet style of Pokemon Black and White.

  • 1 character max per order.
  • Starts at $60 USD for sprite alone, or $80 USD to include a custom scene on a premade background.
  • Examples shown are at 2x size. By default you will receive a 1x and 2x size sprite.
  • The poses here will run the chance of being a little less dynamic than a static sprite in order to allow for more character expression through the animation instead.
  • Complex designs will likely be altered or simplified.
  • Transparent by default. Can be given colored outlines on request.
  • Default text on scenes is either "A wild [name] appeared!" or "Trainer [name] wants to battle!" unless specified.

Premade backgrounds are provided by Snivy101, and additional assets are from the Spriter's Resource.

Trading Card Game GBC styled pixel art

$25 - $35

A limited palette pixel of your character, imitating the art style of Pokemon TCG for Gameboy Color.

  • This commission type has two options: Holo ($25) and Scene ($35).
  • Holo pixels have a default background that emulate the holographic sheen of a card.
  • Scene pixels have more detailed backgrounds that place your character into a specific environment of your choice.
  • This commission type is usually (but not always) limited to 4 colors. I usually pick them myself, but you're free to suggest a palette you'd like.
